Hey! My names Nicholas, but most people call me Nick! I am a student studying at RMIT through OUA. Although this is a entry level subject, I am actually at the end of my study journey, I somehow missed completing this subject when I transferred to OUA.

I am second generation Australian, but my grandparents came to Australia on boat from Italy. In saying that, don't expect me to speak Italian to you!
Over the past 6 years I have been studying part time whilst I grow and run my business, a media production company based in Sydney.

My company specialises in photography and covers events of all different shapes and sizes. From Clubs to Weddings to 70k+ festivals in Australia. The above image is one I took over the weekend at a 20k+ show in Sydney Showgrounds
When I'm not working or studying, you can find me on holidays soaking up the European sun.
