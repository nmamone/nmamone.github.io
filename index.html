My interest in IT started when I was in year 9 at school. I was facinated by how websites were created and work. I started my own mini 'server' from my laptop and experimented with HTML and CSS (kind of like this assignment) and watched tones of YouTube videos on how to get it all working. I created a free domain name and pointed it to my home network and from there was even more intrigued on the networking side of things.

I choose to study at RMIT for its flexibility. I was originally at an on campus university which required me to attend in person, which did not always work with my business.
RMIT offers a more flexible study plan than an on campus uni, and also allows me to go at my own pace.

I expect to broaden my knowledge about IT during my studies, and explore different IT avenues that would otherwise be more difficult on my own. For example, iOS App Development is something that I tried (and failed) to learn over YouTube, but after completing the iOS App Dev unit, with my group we were able to design and deploy a fully featured app with API calls in 12 weeks!
